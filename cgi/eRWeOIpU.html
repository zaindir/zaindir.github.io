How to Reset an Epson L100/300 Printer

  1. Turn on the laptop or PC and the printer to be reset.
  2. Connect the Epson L100/300 printer to the laptop that will be used for resetting.
  3. Extract the downloaded RAR file; this will create a folder.
  4. Open the extracted folder.
  5. Right-click the Adjprog file and select Run as administrator.
  6. Click Particular Adjustment Mode.
  7. Click Waste Ink Pad Counter, then click OK.
  8. Check the Main Pad Counter option.
  9. Click the Initialization button, then click OK.
  10. When the message Please turn off the printer appears, turn off the printer.
  11. Turn the printer back on, then click OK.
