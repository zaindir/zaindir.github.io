How to Reset an Epson L385 Printer

  1. Connect the printer you want to reset to a laptop or PC.
  2. Turn on both the printer and the laptop/PC.
  3. Extract the downloaded RAR file.
  4. Open the extracted folder.
  5. Click the Run-ME file to run the program.
  6. Click OK; the resetter application will open.
  7. Click SELECT.
  8. Select L385 in the Model section, then click OK.
  9. Click Particular Adjustment Mode.
  10. Click Waste Ink Pad Counter, then click OK.
  11. Check the Main Pad Counter option.
  12. Click Initialize, then OK; the reset process will begin.
  13. After the process is complete, turn off the printer and then turn it back on. The printer can now be used normally.
