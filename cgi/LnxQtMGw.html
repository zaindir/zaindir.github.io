How to Reset an Epson L210 Printer

  1. Download the Epson L120 resetter application.
  2. Extract the downloaded application file.
  3. Open the Adjprog application in the extracted folder.
  4. Check the Model section to see if your printer series is already selected. If it says Not Selected, you need to choose your printer series manually.
  5. Click the Select menu; the Select window will open.
  6. Choose your printer series by clicking the Model Name field and selecting L210.
  7. Select the printer Port by clicking the Port field and choosing the port with your printer series, or select Auto Selection to detect the port automatically.
  8. Click OK; the Select window will close.
  9. Click the Particular Adjustment Mode menu at the bottom right; a window with various adjustment and maintenance options will appear.
  10. Select Waste Ink Pad Counter under the Maintenance section.
  11. Click OK; the Waste Ink Pad Counter window will appear.
  12. Check the Main Pad Counter option.
  13. Click the Check button under Check the counter value to display the main pad counter numbers.
  14. Check the Main Pad Counter option again.
  15. Click the Initialize button under Initialize the selected counters.
  16. Click OK in the Information dialog to start the initialization.
  17. Click OK again if a message appears instructing you to turn off the printer.
  18. Turn off the printer by pressing and holding the power button until the power indicator light turns off.
  19. Click OK if an Information dialog appears with instructions to turn on the printer again.
  20. Turn the printer back on by pressing the power button; when the power indicator light is normal, the reset process is complete and the printer is ready to use.
